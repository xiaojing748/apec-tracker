## 摘要

High-Level Dialogue on Women and the Economy

## 全文

On Friday in Shanghai, Huang Xiaowei, Vice President and First Member of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation and Chair of the 2026 APEC Women and the Economy Forum, shared with ministers a statement that reflects the commitments and way forward for APEC to advance women's economic empowerment.

The statement laid out the priorities discussed during the High-Level Policy Dialogue on Women and the Economy and provided policy directions for member economies to support advancing equality and women’s economic empowerment.
